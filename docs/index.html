About BrailleNext

BrailleNext is a revolutionary device designed to assist people with visual disabilities. Our smart glasses, coupled with an Android app, provide real-time visual information through audio feedback.

With BrailleNext, users can capture photos of their surroundings and receive detailed descriptions, read text, and navigate their environment with confidence.

Best of all, BrailleNext is free for personal use!

Key Features

Image Description

Capture photos and receive detailed audio descriptions of the scene.

Text Recognition

Convert printed or digital text into speech for easy comprehension.

Navigation Assistance

Receive guidance and information about your surroundings as you move.

How It Works

  1. Put on the BrailleNext smart glasses
  2. Open the BrailleNext app on your Android device
  3. Connect the app to your smart glasses
  4. Use voice commands or the app interface to capture photos
  5. Listen to the detailed audio description of the captured image or text
